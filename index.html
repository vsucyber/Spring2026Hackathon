Welcome

This collaborative hackathon brings together students from Virginia State University, Northern Virginia Community College, and Brightpoint Community College.

Participants will work in teams to analyze problems, design solutions, and present their ideas. The event emphasizes innovation through Artificial Intelligence and fosters technical creativity.

Objectives

  • Encourage collaboration across Virginia institutions
  • Promote problem solving and innovative technical thinking using Artificial Intelligence
  • Develop practical design, testing, and presentation skills
  • Provide students with real-world project experience

Why Participate?

Gain hands-on experience solving real-world challenges through the use of AI, collaborate with students from other colleges, and build practical technical skills while competing for prizes and recognition.

Event Schedule

  • April 2: Hackathon challenges released
  • April 2 – April 5: Teams work on solutions
  • April 5 – 6:00 PM: Solutions Submission
  • April 6 — 3:00 PM: Solution Presentations
